What is MCEarth: Reforged?

Minecraft Earth shut down in 2021 — but we brought it back. MCEarth: Reforged is a community-driven fork of Vienna used to Revive the Minecraft Earth Servers.

FAQ

Is it free?
Yes! MCEarth: Reforged is completely free for all players.
What is MCEarth: Reforged?
MCEarth: Reforged is an open-source fork of Vienna which recreates the server-side of Minecraft Earth. We use our fork of Vienna Docker to run our server. More info at github.com/MCEarth-Reforged.
Where to download APK?
We do not provide a prepatched APK so you have to patch the official APK yourself.
